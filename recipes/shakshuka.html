Shakshuka: a wonderful Moroccan breakfast

This recipe comes all the way from the northwestern coast of Africa. It is a declious and hearty breakfast that is commonly eaten with some bread. It is savory, a little tangy, full of spices, and healthy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes or about 10 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 whole onion
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • turmeric powder
  • ginger powder
  • salt and pepper (if you want)
  • Oil of your choice

Steps:

  1. Cut the onions, tomatoes, and garlic into small pieces.
  2. Add some oil to the pan and put it on medium or high.
  3. Cook the onions until slightly yellow and soft.
  4. Add the garlic and all the spices, you may want to lower the heat at this point.
  5. After a minute or so, when garlic starts to become a little golden, add the tomatoes.
  6. Cook the tomatoes until they start becoming very soft, add a lid to help this process.
  7. Once the liquid of the tomatoes is starting to evaporate, open 2 spaces up and crack in each egg.
  8. Continue cooking, play with the temperature as needed, close the lid if you desire as well.
  9. When the eggs are to the doneness you prefer, you can stop cooking, add salt and pepper as desired.
  10. Serve on a plate, or ideally, eat it straight from the pan by scooping it with your choice of bread!
